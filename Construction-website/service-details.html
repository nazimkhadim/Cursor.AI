Residential Construction

New builds, custom homes, and residential extensions delivered to the highest standards with full transparency and fixed timelines.

What We Offer

  • New single-family and multi-unit residential builds
  • Custom home design and build from concept to handover
  • Extensions, basements, and loft conversions
  • Fixed-price and cost-plus options with clear documentation

Our Process

We start with a detailed consultation and site assessment, then provide a structured timeline and milestone schedule. Regular updates and a single point of contact keep you informed from foundation to finishing.

Quality & Assurance

All work is carried out by our licensed teams with certified engineers where required. We provide warranties on workmanship and use only approved materials with full documentation.

Confidentiality — Your plans, personal information, and property details are kept strictly confidential. We do not share or reuse your data for any other purpose and comply with applicable privacy standards.

Commercial Construction

Offices, retail, and industrial projects delivered on schedule with full compliance, clear reporting, and a professional project management team.

Scope of Work

  • Office fit-outs and new office builds
  • Retail units, showrooms, and commercial interiors
  • Industrial and warehouse construction
  • Mixed-use and multi-tenant developments

Our Approach

We work to your programme and budget with clear change-order and approval processes. All work is documented for compliance and handover, with as-built and O&M support where needed.

Compliance & Permits

We handle permits, inspections, and regulatory requirements as part of our service. Our team is experienced in building codes and health & safety standards for commercial projects.

Confidentiality — Commercial plans, financial arrangements, and any proprietary information are handled in strict confidence under our standard terms. We do not disclose client or project details to third parties.

Renovation

Structural upgrades, extensions, and full refurbishment carried out with minimal disruption and clear phasing so you know what to expect at every stage.

What We Cover

  • Structural repairs, underpinning, and load-bearing changes
  • Extensions and annexes
  • Full or phased refurbishment (residential and commercial)
  • Heritage and listed building works where applicable

How We Work

We provide a scope document and timeline before work starts. Disruption is minimised with clear site rules and, where possible, phased access. All work is insured and guaranteed as per our terms.

Warranties

Renovation work comes with our standard workmanship warranty. Structural and specialist work is backed by relevant certifications and manufacturer warranties where applicable.

Confidentiality — Your property, designs, and any sensitive information related to the renovation are treated as confidential. We do not use your project for marketing or reference without your written consent.

Interior Design

Cohesive interiors, material selection, and finish specifications aligned with your vision and our construction standards for a seamless result.

What We Provide

  • Material and finish selection (flooring, walls, ceilings, joinery)
  • Layout and space planning for functionality and flow
  • Lighting and MEP coordination where we are on the project
  • Furniture and fixture specifications and procurement support

Process

We work from your brief and existing architecture (or our own build) to propose palettes, samples, and layouts. Revisions are documented so the final design is clear before implementation.

Integration With Build

When we handle both construction and interiors, scheduling and quality are coordinated under one team, reducing gaps and ensuring finishes are installed to the same standards.

Confidentiality — Your design preferences, property information, and any personal or commercial details are kept confidential. We do not share or publish your project without explicit permission.
